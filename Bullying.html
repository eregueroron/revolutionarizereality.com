Stand Up Against Bullying: Create a Safe School for All



Bullying harms everyone in a school by creating a negative environment that disrupts learning and growth. It can lead to severe consequences like anxiety, depression, and suicidal thoughts, making it essential to foster a supportive and respectful atmosphere. Ending bullying ensures students feel safe, respected, and able to succeed academically. To combat bullying, speak up when you see it, support the victims, promote inclusivity, and lead by example with kindness and empathy. By doing so, we can build a stronger, more united school community where everyone feels valued and safe. Let's commit to standing up against bullying and making our school a better place for all.
